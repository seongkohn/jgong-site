Biography

Jingmian Gong is a composer whose music has been performed by ensembles including Ensemble Dal Niente, the Lydian String Quartet, Lamnth, Hub New Music, and Collage New Music, which premiered her commissioned work during her 2024–25 fellowship with the ensemble. Her recent commissions include works for the Lingnan Normal University Choir as well as Boston-based new music performers and ensembles.

Gong is an Assistant Professor at Berklee College of Music. She also serves on the faculty of Walnut Hill School for the Arts and as an Instructor at Brandeis University. She previously studied composition at the Boston Conservatory at Berklee (BM) and Brandeis University (MFA), where she is completing her PhD in Music Composition and Theory.
